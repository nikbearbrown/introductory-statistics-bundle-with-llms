## Overview

**Audience.** This book is written for the undergraduate taking their first college statistics course, typically in their second year. They are not math majors. They are studying psychology, biology, business, education, nursing, sociology, criminal justice, economics, public health, or any of the fields where the math of inference is a working tool, not the subject. They have had at least one college math course (often college algebra or pre-calc); some have had calculus.
